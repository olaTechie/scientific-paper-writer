# PRISMA-IPD Checklist

Reference: Stewart LA, Clarke M, Rovers M, et al. Preferred reporting items
for a systematic review and meta-analysis of individual participant data:
the PRISMA-IPD statement. *JAMA*. 2015;313(16):1657-1665.

This checklist adapts the standard PRISMA items for the specific requirements
of IPD meta-analyses. Items marked with an asterisk (*) are new or
substantially modified from the standard PRISMA checklist.

## Usage Notes

When completing this checklist for a manuscript:
- Replace `[ ]` with `[x]` for items that are reported
- Flag items not reported with a note explaining why
- Items marked with * are IPD-specific; all others follow standard PRISMA 2020
- Include this checklist as a supplementary table in the manuscript submission
